About This Page

This website is dedicated to showing and sharing the beauty of the ancient East African country name Ethiopia. Believed to be the crattle of humanity, Ethiopia bousts a wild range of cultural diversity, delicous food, amazinig mountains and ancient history.

Ethiopia, located in the Horn of Africa, is a country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the cradle of humanity, it is home to ancient archaeological sites, including the famed rock-hewn churches of Lalibela and the obelisks of Axum.


Ethiopia has diverse landscapes range from the rugged peaks of the Simien Mountains to the otherworldly Danakil Depression, one of the hottest places on Earth.
